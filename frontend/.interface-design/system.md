# Interface Design System

## Direction & Feel
- Reception ledger aesthetic: warm, calm, and operational.
- Surfaces: parchment/ivory with soft borders.
- Accent: deep forest green for primary actions and key states.
- Secondary warmth: brass/gold highlights for emphasis and “today”.
- Typography: editorial headline + modern sans body.

## Depth Strategy
- Subtle shadows on major panels only; borders for structure elsewhere.
- No dramatic elevation jumps.

## Spacing Base Unit
- Base: 8px (use multiples for padding and gaps).

## Key Component Patterns
- Buttons: pill shape, forest primary, ivory text; secondary in parchment.
- Tags/States: soft-tinted rectangles with thin borders.
- Calendar: occupancy “lanes” (short rounded bars) instead of dots.
- Modals: ivory surface, soft border, gentle shadow.
- Reception header: framed hero card with eyebrow + serif title + live badge, and right-side quick stats cards.
- Filter rail: titled filter card with active-filter counter and applied-filter chips summary.
- Feedback messaging: inline alerts for errors; global success toast anchored at bottom-right (mobile full-width bottom), longer duration for post-redirect visibility.
- Loading state: prominent inline loading chip with spinner + subtle pulse animation (not plain small text) for operational lists.
